Meet the CodeQuest Team

Passionate students collaborating to make front-end development accessible, engaging, and fun for everyone.

Our Story

CodeQuest began as a collaborative group project for our Version Control System course at Islington College, evolving into something much more meaningful.

Project Conception

We decided to create a visually engaging educational portfolio that would showcase not only our collaborative development skills but also our proficiency with Git and GitHub workflows.

Team Formation

Five passionate students came together, each bringing unique skills and perspectives to create something that would help others learn web development.

Vision Realized

What started as a course requirement evolved into a comprehensive learning platform designed to make front-end development education more interactive and accessible.
